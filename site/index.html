Internet

From $0 local

Five layers of connectivity. Something always works — from next door to the other side of the world. Each layer is independent. Lose one, the others keep going.

BLE Mesh (0–100m)Phone-to-phone. Every device with Bluetooth is a relay node. Free. Works with no infrastructure at all. Try it in your browser →
WiFi Mesh (0–300m)Pi nodes create local hotspots. Connect like normal WiFi. Stream, browse, share.
LoRa (2–15km)Long-range radio. Text and small data. $10 per module. No SIM, no licence, no tower. Solar powered.
Yggdrasil OverlayEncrypted mesh network that runs on top of any internet connection. End-to-end, no central server.
4G FallbackSilent.link eSIM for when you need the global internet. No name attached. Data only.
HardwarePi Zero 2 W ($25) · LoRa SX1262 ($10) · Solar kit ($15) · Pico W relay ($6)
